Welcome to my blog! This is the first post in a journey of sharing knowledge, experiences, and reflections on software development, interface design, and technology in general.

## Why a blog?

I believe writing is one of the best ways to solidify learning. Over the years working as a full-stack developer, I've accumulated many experiences that deserve to be documented and shared.

## What to expect

Here you'll find:

- Practical tutorials on **Next.js**, **React**, **Vue.js**, and other technologies
- Reflections on software architecture and best practices
- Productivity tips and tools
- Real-world project experiences

## About this site

This blog is part of my personal portfolio, built with **Next.js**, **Tailwind CSS**, and managed with **DecapCMS**. Content is versioned on GitHub and automatically published via Cloudflare Pages.

Feel free to explore and, if you want, get in touch via social media!
